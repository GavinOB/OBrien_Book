\section{Edith A. (Wickens) Giguere}

\MainPerson{Edith A.\textsuperscript{4} Wickens} (\Lineage{3}{Frances}, \Lineage{2}{Michael}, \Lineage{1}{William}) was born in Boston, Suffolk County, Massachusetts, on 22 July 1894.\cite{Edith4WickensBirth} She died in Detroit, Wayne County, Michigan, on 9 February 1957.\cite{Edith4WickensDeath} She married, in Boston on 12 October 1913, \MainPerson{George Hector Giguere}.\cite{Edith4WickensMarriage} He was born in Holyoke, Hampden County, Massachusetts, on 25 February 1893 to John B.\ Giguere and Corinne (Lecours) Giguere.\cite{GeorgeGiguereBirth} He died in Detroit, Wayne County, Michigan, on 19 July 1974.\cite{GeorgeGiguereDeath}

By 1930 the family had moved from Boston to Detroit. George was working as a consulting engineer. The family owned their home, worth \$11,000, at 13002 Greiner Ave.\cite{Census1930GeorgeGiguere}
